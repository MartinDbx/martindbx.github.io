Summary

I am a PhD student supervised by Maja Volkov from the Mathematics Department of the University of Mons (UMONS). My PhD is funded by the F.R.S.-FNRS. I am interested in arithmetic dynamics, currently working on Lubin's conjecture using p-adic Hodge theory.
